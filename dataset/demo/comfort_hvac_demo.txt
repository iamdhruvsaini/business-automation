Date: January 19, 2026
Participants: Sarah (Agent Sales), Lisa (Comfort Heating and Cooling Manager)

Sarah: Hi Lisa, tell me about Comfort Heating and Cooling.

Lisa: Hi Sarah. We're based in Denver, Colorado. We specialize in heating and cooling systems - furnace installation and repair, air conditioning, boiler service, heat pumps, and we also do air duct cleaning. We've been serving the Denver area for 12 years.

Sarah: What are your business hours?

Lisa: Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM Mountain Time. We also have Saturday hours from 9 AM to 2 PM for emergencies and urgent calls. Our office is at 456 Mountain View Drive, Denver, CO 80202.

Sarah: How do you define an emergency?

Lisa: In Colorado, no heat in winter is definitely an emergency - it can get dangerously cold. Same with no AC during summer heat waves. Also gas leaks, carbon monoxide alarms, or any strange smells from heating equipment. Those are all urgent.

Sarah: Emergency call routing?

Lisa: For emergencies, first try our on-call tech line at 303-555-7777. That rotates between our three senior techs. If no answer within 25 seconds, try our owner Jim directly at 303-555-6666. He wants to know about every emergency.

Sarah: And standard call handling?

Lisa: Daytime calls go to 303-555-0000 - that's our main line. After hours non-emergencies, take a message with name, number, address, and the issue. Also ask if they have a service contract with us - we prioritize those customers.

Sarah: Anything you don't service?

Lisa: We don't work on window AC units - only central systems. We also don't do refrigeration for commercial kitchens. And we're residential plus light commercial only, no industrial.

Sarah: Service area?

Lisa: Denver metro area and surrounding communities. We go as far as Boulder to the north, Castle Rock to the south. About a 35-mile radius from downtown Denver.

Sarah: Any special notes?

Lisa: We're a Carrier authorized dealer, so mention that if someone asks about quality. And we offer financing on new system installations - that's helpful for people facing big expenses.
