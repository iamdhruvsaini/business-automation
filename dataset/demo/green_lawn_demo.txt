DEMO CALL TRANSCRIPT - GREEN LAWN CARE
Date: January 18, 2026
Participants: Sarah (Clara Sales), Robert (Green Lawn Care Owner)

---

Sarah: Hi Robert, thanks for your time today. What can you tell me about Green Lawn Care?

Robert: Hey Sarah. We're a landscaping and lawn maintenance company in Dallas, Texas. We do weekly lawn mowing, fertilization, weed control, tree trimming, landscape design, sprinkler system installation and repair, and seasonal cleanups.

Sarah: Nice range of services. What are your operating hours?

Robert: We're a bit different because our crews start early. Office hours are Monday through Friday, 7 AM to 4 PM Central Time. Saturday we have a half day, 8 AM to noon for office calls. Our office is at 321 Oak Street, Dallas, TX 75201.

Sarah: How do you handle urgent situations? Is there such a thing as a lawn care emergency?

Robert: Ha, you'd be surprised. Emergencies for us are broken sprinkler lines causing flooding, irrigation system failures during a heat wave, or fallen tree limbs blocking driveways. Those need quick attention.

Sarah: Got it. How should those calls be routed?

Robert: For emergencies, call our field supervisor Carlos at 214-555-3333. He manages the crews and can dispatch someone quickly. If Carlos doesn't answer, try me at 214-555-4444. We try to respond within an hour for true emergencies.

Sarah: Regular call handling?

Robert: During office hours, transfer to 214-555-0000. Our receptionist Maria handles everything. After hours for non-emergencies, take a message. We need name, phone, address, and whether they're a current customer or new. For new customers, ask how they heard about us.

Sarah: Any services you don't do?

Robert: We don't do hardscaping - no patios, retaining walls, or concrete work. And we don't handle pest control. If someone has a pest problem, refer them to a pest control company.

Sarah: Anything else?

Robert: Our service area is Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex only. About a 30-mile radius from downtown Dallas. And mention that we offer free estimates for new customers - that gets people interested.
